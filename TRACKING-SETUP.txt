Local testing:
Analytics is automatically disabled on localhost, 127.0.0.1, [::1], and *.local.
The live patterndesk.co.uk site continues to send analytics normally.

After deployment:
1. Open GA4 > Reports > Realtime.
2. Use the live site and perform one quiz answer, one game round, and one guide click.
3. Confirm the custom events appear. Realtime/DebugView is quickest; standard reports can take up to 24 hours.
4. In GA4 Admin > Events / Key events, mark the events you consider conversions, such as sign_up, quiz_complete, game_round_complete, and checkout_click.
